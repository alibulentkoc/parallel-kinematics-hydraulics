PKM Electrohydraulic Parallel Kinematics Testbed

Planar 2-DOF / 3-DOF electrohydraulic parallel manipulator — instructor & student simulation.

Applications

Self-guided course: learn electrohydraulic parallel kinematics by working through the simulator, lesson by lesson.
 📐 Engineering Handbook →
The theory underneath: math of motion, hydraulic design calculations, control system, and electrical/control wiring.
 Instructor Console →
Full authority: presets, live parameters, fault injection, validation checklist, answer-key comparison, log export.

Locked geometry, assignment objectives, tuning controls, formative self-check, canonical submission export.
 Grading Console →
Drop in a student submission (+ optional answer key); rubric-scored report. Grades sim & hardware logs identically.
 Visualization Demo →
Minimal harness: draggable target, manipulability heatmap, pressure-lit legs, strip charts.
