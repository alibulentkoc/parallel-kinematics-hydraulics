# Quizzes

Six asset-grounded quizzes that verify the understanding behind the course artifacts. Every
question ties to a competency and an artifact; at least half are built on a figure, exported
plot, ISO 1219 schematic, or rig/twin log — never pure recall.

| Quiz | Topic | Lessons | Competencies |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Quiz 1](quiz-1-kinematics.md) | Kinematics | 1.1–1.4 | C2–C4 |
| [Quiz 2](quiz-2-hydraulic-sizing.md) | Hydraulic sizing (ISO 1219) | 2.1–2.4 | C5 |
| [Quiz 3](quiz-3-pwm-position-control.md) | PWM & position control | 2.5–2.6 | C6–C7 |
| [Quiz 4](quiz-4-3dof-manipulability.md) | 3-DOF, manipulability, singularities | 3.1–3.4 | C10–C12 |
| [Quiz 5](quiz-5-coordinated-tuning.md) | Coordinated control & tuning | 3.5–3.6 | C13–C14 |
| [Quiz 6](quiz-6-twin-validation.md) | Twin validation & diagnosis | 4.1–4.4 | C15–C16 |

Standards items (ISO 1219 / ANSI Y32.10 symbol interpretation and schematic reading) appear in
Quizzes 2–3, where the fluid-power graphics live. Validation and fault-diagnosis items in
Quiz 6 are anchored to the exported accuracy report and discrepancy signatures.
