# Reference Handbook

The handbook is the **engineering reference** behind the lesson-based curriculum. The
modules teach the concepts one small step at a time; these chapters give the fuller,
continuous treatment — the derivations, design calculations, and worked numbers you
reach for once you understand the ideas.

## Chapters

- [Kinematics & the Math of Motion](01-kinematics-and-motion.md) — inverse/forward
  kinematics, the Jacobian, manipulability, and singularities of the 2-RPR geometry.
- [Hydraulic Design & Calculations](02-hydraulic-design.md) — cylinder sizing, area
  asymmetry, valve flow, and pump/relief selection with real numbers.
- [The Control System](03-control-system.md) — the PID controller, anti-windup,
  feedforward, trajectory generation, and the fixed-timestep loop.
- [Electrical & Control Wiring](04-electrical-and-control-wiring.md) — the three
  domains, sensors and drivers, and the safety chain (descriptive engineering).
- [Worked Design Example](05-design-worked-example.md) — a full pass through sizing
  and checking the default machine end to end.
